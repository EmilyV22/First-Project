Garden

In this section I would like to talk about what I want to grow in my garden. I would like to grow things such as:

  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumbers
  • Zucchini
  • Peppers (both hot and mild)
  • Green beans
  • Winter Squash

Honestly that is a very small list compared to everything that I could grow, but those are pretty much the staples. Anything else is pretty much just for fun. I do enjoy occasionally throwing in something that I wouldn't normally grow. It helps to keep thing interesting. It also helps to include different varieties of the staples. I especially love all the varieties of tomatoes that are available.
